    What is it about?

    Our text encryptor is an innovative tool designed to transform simple messages into encrypted codes, keeping them safe and secure. As part of the Oracle Next Education, Group 7 program, this project challenges participants to combine creativity with logic, building an application that is not only functional, but also intuitive and visually appealing.

    Why is it important?

    Encryption is essential for data protection in the modern era. This project will allow you to understand the basics of digital security, explore encryption algorithms, and apply this knowledge in a practical solution you can proudly showcase. Additionally, it presents an excellent opportunity to collaborate with other group members, share ideas, and learn together.

    ¡How to Use Our Text Encryptor!

    • Enter your message in the text field.
    • Click  Encrypt  to encrypt your message.
    • Check the encrypted message in the results window.
    • Click   Decrypt  to decrypt.
    • Click  Copy  to copy the encrypted message.
    • Click  Reset  to clear the fields.
